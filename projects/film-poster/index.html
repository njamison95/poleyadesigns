Mason Film Festival Poster 2025

Contest Winner

On March 7, 2025, I was awarded winner of the 2025 Mason Film Festival Poster Contest. The task was to create a poster that related to film and that followed the university branding guidelines. The final poster was featured on the Film Mason's newsletter, website, and social media platforms.

My first step to design this poster was to review the GMU brand guides. I downloaded all the required fonts and kept note of the required colors. Then I conducted research on film imagery, with a focus on film wheels and strips. I experimented with typography, contrast, balance, and color in my early drafts as shown below.

My final idea was to have film strips weave between the letters of the title, the latter of which I divided and placed in opposite directions at the edges the poster so they would bleed off the sides. I decided to use darker and lighter shades of the official Mason green to both create more contrast between the title in Mason gold and to distinguish it from other GMU media while still evoking the brand. As a designer, I aim to create works that are driven by empathy. For this work, I wanted to elicit the feelings of excitement and playfulness that one generally feels when attending a festival.

I'd like to extend my gratitude to the Film and Video Studies department at George Mason University for honoring my work with this award.
