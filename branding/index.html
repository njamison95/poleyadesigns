BRANDING

Educating Caldwell Road

Educating Caldwell Road is a school in Liberia founded by Elizabeth Bowen that aims to empower its students through community-oriented education.


As a new school, it needed a visual identity that reflected its values and distinct approach to teaching. The concept features bright, bold colors and a bird logo turned backward to symbolize the founder giving back to her community.

Poleya Designs

Poleya Designs is the personal brand I created as an authentic expression of who I am both as a designer and a person.


Rooted in empathy and resilience, it embodies my values and creative evolution. I chose the moth as my logo to represent perserverence, transformation, and the pursuit of light even in the darkest moments. It is a reminder that the journey holds as much meaning as the destination.
