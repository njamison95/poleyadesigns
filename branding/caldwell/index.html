Educating Caldwell Road Poleya Designs George Mason University

Educating Caldwell Road is a school in Liberia founded by Elizabeth Bowen that aims to empower its students through community-oriented education.


As a new school, it needed a visual identity that reflected its values and distinct approach to teaching. The visual identity features bright, bold colors and a bird logo turned backward to symbolize the founder giving back to her community.
